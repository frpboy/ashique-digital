# Ashique's 4-Step Growth Process

Ashique follows a rigorous, data-backed 4-step framework to ensure every client achieves predictable growth.

### 1. Discovery (The Audit)
Before writing a single line of code or launching an ad, Ashique performs a deep-dive audit of your current marketing assets.
- Identifying leaks in the funnel.
- Analyzing past campaign data.
- Defining the "Ideal Customer Profile" (ICP).

### 2. Strategy (The Blueprint)
Design the full growth system architecture.
- Brand positioning adjustment.
- Funnel mapping (AIDA framework).
- Choosing the right traffic channels (Google vs Meta).
- Setting up the "15-Point Lead Gen Audit" checklist.

### 3. Execution (The Build)
Building the high-converting infrastructure.
- High-fidelity landing page development.
- Ad campaign setup & creative direction.
- CRM & Tracking integration (Sanity, PostHog, UPSTASH).
- Resend email automation setup.

### 4. Optimize (The Scale)
Continuous improvement based on real-world data.
- A/B testing headlines and CTAs.
- Scaling winning campaigns.
- Reporting on ROI and ROAS.
- Iterative strategy adjustments.

## Why This Works
Most agencies skip steps 1 and 2 and jump straight to 3. By engineering the strategy first, Ashique ensures your ad budget isn't wasted on a broken funnel.
