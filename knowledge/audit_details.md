# 15-Point Lead Generation & Growth Audit

This is Ashique's high-value framework for SMEs and startups to identify revenue leaks and scale predictable systems.

### What is in the Audit?
The audit is a 19-page deep dive covering 15 critical points across three main pillars:

#### Pillar 1: High-Performance Funnels
1. **The Core Offer Hook**: Is the value proposition clear in 3 seconds?
2. **Friction Analysis**: Eliminating fields and clicks that kill conversions.
3. **Trust Architecture**: Placement of social proof, case studies, and badges.
4. **Mobile Responsiveness**: Speed and UX optimization for the 80% of mobile users.
5. **Post-Opt-In Experience**: The automation sequence that warms up cold leads.

#### Pillar 2: Ad Strategy (Google & Meta)
6. **Audience Intent Mapping**: Targeting based on pain points, not just demographics.
7. **Creative Vitality**: Testing static vs. video vs. user-generated content (UGC).
8. **ROAS vs. MER**: Measuring "Marketing Efficiency Ratio" for true profit tracking.
9. **Retargeting Loops**: Capturing the 98% who don't convert on the first visit.
10. **Budget Allocation**: Shifting spend to high-performing "winning" concepts.

#### Pillar 3: Lead Quality & Sales Alignment
11. **Lead Scoring**: Separating curiosity seekers from ready-to-buy clients.
12. **The "Wait Time" Metric**: Reducing the gap between lead capture and first touch.
13. **Nurture Depth**: Providing value before asking for the sale.
14. **Data Attribution**: Knowing exactly which rupee generated which conversion.
15. **Scaling Roadmap**: The step-by-step plan to 10x lead volume without breaking the system.
