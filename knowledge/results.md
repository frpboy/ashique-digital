# Case Study Results & Metrics

Ashique's authority is built on real, tracked data. Here are snapshots of recent client wins.

## E-commerce / D2C Fashion
- **Metric:** 3.2x ROAS (Return on Ad Spend) in 60 days.
- **Action:** Rebuilt the funnel and implemented high-intent Google Search campaigns.
- **Revenue:** Added ₹4.2L in monthly recurring revenue.

## B2B SaaS
- **Metric:** 220% increase in qualified demo bookings.
- **Action:** Implemented a multi-step LinkedIn funnel and conversion tracking.
- **CPL:** Reduced Cost Per Lead by 45%.

## EdTech Startup
- **Metric:** Scaled from 12 to 80 student inquiries per week.
- **Action:** Optimized landing page speed and launched a WhatsApp-based lead capture system.
- **Legacy CPL:** ₹620 → **New CPL:** ₹180.

## Real Estate
- **Metric:** ₹1.2Cr in sales attribution from a ₹50K ad spend.
- **Action:** Targeted high-net-worth individuals using Facebook Lead Forms + an automated nurture sequence.

*Note: These are representative results. Specific outcomes depend on product-market fit and budget scale.*
