# Services Offered by Ashique

Ashique provides full-stack growth consulting for businesses looking to scale their lead generation and brand authority.

### 1. Brand Strategy & Positioning
- **What:** Reframing how your business is perceived in the market.
- **Outcome:** Move from a commodity to a category leader.

### 2. Full-Stack Lead Generation Systems
- **What:** Building end-to-end pipelines that capture and nurture leads.
- **Outcome:** Steady, predictable flow of qualified inquiries.

### 3. Sales Funnel Design
- **What:** High-converting landing pages and multi-step inquiry flows.
- **Outcome:** Higher conversion rates and lower cost-per-lead (CPL).

### 4. Paid Traffic Management (Ads)
- **What:** Expert management of Google Ads and Meta (Facebook/Instagram) Ads.
- **Outcome:** Scalable traffic with a focus on ROAS (Return on Ad Spend).

### 5. Tracking, Data & Attribution
- **What:** Implementing professional tracking (PostHog, Vercel Analytics).
- **Outcome:** Total clarity on where your customers are coming from.

### 6. Content Distribution Strategy
- **What:** LinkedIn authority building and "Hub-and-Spoke" content systems.
- **Outcome:** Earned authority and lower customer acquisition costs.

## Engagement Model
- **Consultancy:** Strategic advice and roadmapping.
- **Implementation:** Full build-out of funnels and systems.
- **Retainer:** Ongoing optimization and scaling.
