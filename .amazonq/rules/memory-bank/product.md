# Product Overview

## Project Identity
**ashique.digital** is a dynamic personal brand and portfolio website designed to convert visitors into booked discovery calls for Ashique, a Brand Strategist and Lead Generation Consultant.

## Purpose & Value Proposition
Position Ashique as a strategic growth partner (not a commodity freelancer) while actively generating and capturing leads 24/7 without manual effort. The platform showcases proof of results through case studies and scales from solo freelancer to agency without rebuilding.

## Key Features & Capabilities

### Lead Generation Engine
- **AI-Powered Chat Widget**: Floating chat orb with Gemini 1.5 Flash + Pinecone RAG that answers questions about services, process, pricing, and results with streamed responses
- **Cal.com Integration**: Embedded discovery call booking throughout the site for frictionless conversion
- **Lead Magnet System**: Free audit landing page with PDF delivery via Resend and automatic email list addition
- **Contact Forms**: Multi-purpose forms with instant email notifications to Ashique

### Content Management
- **Sanity.io CMS**: Headless CMS for publishing case studies, blog posts, testimonials, and service updates without developer intervention
- **Dynamic Case Studies**: Detailed client success stories with metrics, strategy breakdowns, and testimonials
- **Insights Blog**: SEO-optimized blog with tags, categories, and related posts functionality
- **Portable Text**: Rich content editing with visual editor for non-technical content updates

### Conversion-Optimized Pages
- **Homepage**: Hero with dual CTAs, trust indicators, problem statement, services grid, featured case studies, 4-step process, testimonials, and final CTA
- **Services**: 6 detailed service offerings (Brand Strategy, Digital Marketing Strategy, Lead Generation Systems, Funnel Design, Paid Ads Strategy, Growth Consulting)
- **About**: Professional bio, philosophy, credentials, and inline CTAs
- **Case Studies**: Grid view and dynamic detail pages with full client journey documentation
- **Contact**: Multiple conversion paths with Cal.com embed and contact form
- **Free Audit**: Standalone lead magnet landing page optimized for conversions

### Performance & SEO
- Lighthouse score ≥ 90 on all pages
- LCP < 2.5 seconds
- Full mobile responsiveness (375px–1440px)
- Automated metadata generation, sitemap.xml, and robots.txt
- WCAG 2.1 AA accessibility compliance

## Target Users

### Primary Persona
SME owners (₹1Cr–₹20Cr revenue) struggling with lead generation who need strategic growth partnership

### Secondary Persona
Startup founders needing go-to-market digital strategy and brand positioning

### Tertiary Persona
Marketing managers at mid-size companies seeking consulting and strategic guidance

## Technical Foundation
Built on Next.js 16 with React 19, deployed on Vercel's free tier with 99.9% uptime guarantee. Integrates Sanity.io for content, Gemini AI for conversational intelligence, and Resend for transactional emails.
